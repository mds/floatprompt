## 💳 Credit Card Deposit Protocol

*"I do not get off of a Rocket selling system call without getting some dollar on a credit card as a deposit."*

### Primary Commitment Standard

**Professional Requirement:**
Every successful sales conversation must end with financial commitment, not just verbal agreement.

**Quality Standard:**
"You didn't make a sale just 'cause somebody said they wanna move forward with you."

### Credit Card Deposit Script

**Standard Closing Script:**
"Perfect. I just texted you the link. Click that link. I'll be on the call with you. Just enter your credit card that puts a deposit that'll kick off a notification to my team. They'll follow up and provide invoice, and also send you follow up paperwork for us to sign. Contract."

**Implementation Requirements:**
- Have payment system ready and accessible
- Stay on call during payment process
- Guide them through payment completion
- Confirm transaction and next steps

### Professional Closing Language

**Direct Asking Techniques:**
- "Does that make sense? Does that credit card start with a four or five?"
- "Do you have the money on you?"
- "Let's get that"
- "Sign here"

**Professional Standard:**
"There's nothing weird about it. There's nothing they ask for the sale. That's what a salesperson does."

**Option 1: Credit Card Deposit (Primary)**

## 🔄 Price Delivery and Movement

*"When I do the offer, I move on. When people go, how much is it? I say, well, it's only $25,000. But because we understand that for you to really grow, you need to train up your team, we also allow you to add two extra team members at no additional fee."*

### Price Delivery Protocol

**Price Statement:**
- State price clearly: "Well, it's only $25,000"
- Use "only" to minimize psychological impact
- Immediately move to additional value

**Movement Pattern:**
"See how I moved on once I said the price, I moved on from it."

**Professional Behavior:**
- Never stay on price discussion
- Add value immediately after price statement
- Guide conversation toward decision and commitment
- Maintain professional confidence throughout

### Value Addition Technique

**Standard Pattern:**
"But because we understand that [specific benefit relevant to their situation], we also [additional value] at no additional fee."

**Implementation:**
- Reference their specific needs from discovery
- Add relevant value that addresses concerns
- Make additional value feel substantial
- Connect to their success requirements

## 📞 Advanced Closing Scenarios

### Deposit with Demonstration Requirements

**Scenario:**
Prospect requests demonstration before commitment.

**Professional Response:**
"Here's how we work, because I'm gonna have a lot of these conversations is if you put a deposit, you get your name put down. So now you're in the queue, and then we'll follow up and we'll do the demo. We'll customize it for your situation, and if for whatever reason you decide at that point it's not a fit, then we'll just refund the deposit. Does that make sense?"

**Key Elements:**
- Deposit required for queue position
- Demonstration follows deposit, not precedes
- Customization and personalization promised
- Risk reversal with refund guarantee
- Professional process enforcement

### Professional Closing Persistence

**Multiple Ask Strategy:**
- First ask: Direct closing attempt
- Second ask: Handle objection and re-close
- Third ask: Alternative commitment (BAMFAM)
- Professional standard: Never end without commitment

**Escalation Protocol:**
- Increase directness with each ask
- Reference urgency and scarcity factors
- Maintain professional confidence throughout
- Willing to enforce boundaries if necessary
