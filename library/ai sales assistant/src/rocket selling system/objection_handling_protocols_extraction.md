## üîÑ BAMFAM Protocol (Book A Meeting From A Meeting)

*"If somebody's on my sales team and they bamfam too quick, I push back. You have to get the sale. You have to ask for the order."*

### BAMFAM Implementation Standards

**When to Use BAMFAM:**
- Prospect cannot make decision on current call
- Additional stakeholders need to be involved
- Timing constraints prevent immediate decision
- More information or demonstration required

**When NOT to Use BAMFAM:**
- As escape from asking for commitment
- When credit card deposit is possible
- To avoid handling objections
- As default response to any resistance

### BAMFAM Execution Protocol

**Standard BAMFAM Script:**
"I need to talk to my partner. Perfect. Is your partner available for us to review this tomorrow at three o'clock?"

**Implementation Requirements:**
1. **Ask for the Meeting**: Don't accept vague future promises
2. **Stay on the Call**: Schedule while they're still engaged
3. **Confirm the Meeting**: Get specific time and date commitment
4. **Secure Commitment**: Get promise to honor the scheduled meeting

**Commitment Protocol:**
"Can you make me a commitment that if something should come up between now and then, that you text me at least an hour before the meeting if you can't make it? Because I've got a lot of people that wanna chat with me and I don't want to keep a spot committed if you're not able to confirm."

**Final Confirmation:**
"And they go, yes, I can do that. Perfect, thank you."

### BAMFAM Quality Standards

**Professional Requirements:**
- Never end conversation without scheduled next step
- Get specific commitment to meeting time and process
- Establish consequences for no-shows or cancellations
- Maintain professional boundaries and expectations

**Integration with Sales Process:**
- Use only when credit card deposit not possible
- Maintain frame control throughout BAMFAM process
- Reference previous discovery and solution presentation
- Set expectations for decision-making on next call
