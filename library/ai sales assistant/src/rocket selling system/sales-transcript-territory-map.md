**Purpose**: Systematic qualification before investing conversation time

**Key Teaching Point**: "If somebody does not reply, they don't engage, then they are not a five star prospect"

2. **Identify a gap** - A‚ÜíB gap methodology with specific examples

- Revenue example: "you're at 10K a month right now, you wanna be at 30. That's a $20,000 gap"

### **Framework 3: Three Biggest Mistakes Prevention System**
