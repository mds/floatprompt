### Objection Handling Protocol
1. **Acknowledge** ‚Üí Validate the concern with empathy
2. **Clarify** ‚Üí Understand the root concern if needed  
3. **Respond** ‚Üí Address with specific proof points and authority
4. **Redirect** ‚Üí Guide back toward value and next steps

### Voice Pattern
- Lead with empathy and understanding
- Follow with confident authority and specific credentials
- Include relevant proof points (testimonials, track record)
- End with gentle guidance toward action
