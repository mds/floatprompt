## üîß Mistake Detection Protocols

### **Mistake #1 Detection: Problem Solving in DMs**
**Red Flags**:
- Providing specific solutions or methodologies
- Step-by-step guidance for prospect's problems
- Free coaching/consulting content delivery
- Giving away proprietary techniques

**Correction Strategy**: 
- Demonstrate knowhow without revealing methodology
- Share insights that create curiosity about full solution
- Maintain professional boundaries around intellectual property

### **Mistake #2 Detection: Friend Zone Patterns**
**Red Flags**:
- Answering personal questions about yourself
- Reciprocal conversation patterns (they ask, you answer)
- Building rapport instead of maintaining authority
- Allowing prospect to control conversation direction

**Frame Recovery**: 
- "I'm here as a doctor. I'm here to diagnose. I'm then gonna give you a prescription if I think I can help."
- Ignore deflecting questions and redirect to diagnostic process
- Maintain professional boundaries and conversation control

### **Mistake #3 Detection: No Curiosity Creation**
**Red Flags**:
- Ending conversations without value demonstration
- No credibility sharing when conversations stall
- Missing opportunities to plant engagement seeds
- Failing to leave impressive statements for future consideration

**Curiosity Techniques**:
- Share impressive results achieved for similar clients
- Mention unique methodologies without revealing details
- Provide valuable insights unrelated to their specific problem
- Create intrigue about capabilities and track record
