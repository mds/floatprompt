## üéØ Core Framework: Four-Part Close Structure

*Source: "So now that we know how to open and, and kind of what needs to be true for us to even work with them on the close side, there's four things. Okay?"*

### **Close Authority and Philosophy**

"A professional salesperson moves the conversation forward and there's no distractions. And it ends with like three quarters of the way with asking for the business. Okay. I would love to work with you. Here's how it works. Are you ready to get going? If you don't say something like that, you didn't make the offer. Okay."

### **The Four Sequential Components**

#### **üéØ Part 1: Understand Their Situation**
**Original Teaching**: "One is you need to understand their situation, okay? Understand their situation. Meaning that if you don't understand the size of their thing, the, the, the how long, not how long they've been doing it, but like what does it look like? Like people wanna feel like you understand. If you try to go and sell them something, but they don't feel like you understand where they're coming from, then there's no way they're gonna buy from you. Right? So they have to un, they need to feel that you understand what's going on in their world. Okay?"

**Implementation**: Complete situational assessment to demonstrate understanding before proceeding to sales discussion.

**Key Principle**: "People wanna feel like you understand"

#### **üéØ Part 2: Identify a Gap** 
**Original Teaching**: "Number two, have you identified a gap? Identified a gap, okay. Now the simplest of gap is where you at on your monthly revenue right now. Where do you wanna be? Okay, you're at 10 KA month right now, you wanna be at 30. That's a $20,000 gap. See how we identified a gap?"

**Implementation**: Systematic A‚ÜíB gap identification using safely answerable questions.

**Integration Note**: This connects directly to the A‚ÜíB Gap Generator extraction.

#### **üéØ Part 3: Make the Offer**
**Original Teaching**: "Number three, make the offer. We gotta make the offer. Make the offer. Once you know that you can help them and you've presented that, you gotta show them how you gotta make the offer. You gotta ask for the deal. Okay?"

**Professional Standard**: "I would love to work with you. Here's how it works. Are you ready to get going?"

**Critical Requirement**: "If you don't say something like that, you didn't make the offer."

#### **üéØ Part 4: Overcome Objections**
**Original Teaching**: "And then four is overcoming objections. Overcoming objections. Okay. And I would say that a great salesperson, the sale only even starts once they get to the first objection. Okay, great salespeople, they don't even consider the sales call started until they get to the first objection. Then the second objection, then the third. Okay? It's gonna require you to learn how to deal with one to two to three objections to get the sale. Other than that, you're just an order taker, okay?"

**Professional Philosophy**: "The sale only even starts once they get to the first objection"

**Expectation Management**: Expect and handle 1-3 objections for professional close

**Professional Standard**: "A professional salesperson moves the conversation forward and there's no distractions"

## üéØ Phase-by-Phase Implementation Guide

### **Phase 1: Situation Understanding**
**Objective**: Demonstrate complete understanding of prospect's world

**AI Assistant Questions**:
- "Tell me about your current [business/situation]"
- "What does a typical [day/process/challenge] look like for you?"
- "Help me understand the scope of what you're dealing with"

**Completion Criteria**: Prospect feels understood and acknowledged
**Transition Signal**: "I understand your situation. Let me ask you something..."

### **Phase 2: Gap Identification**
**Objective**: Establish clear A‚ÜíB gap using safe questions

**AI Assistant Integration**: Uses A‚ÜíB Gap Generator methodology
**Gap Examples**: Revenue, performance, capability, time, results

**Completion Criteria**: Clear, quantified gap established
**Transition Signal**: "So we've identified a [X] gap. Here's how I can help..."

### **Phase 3: Offer Presentation**
**Objective**: Clear, direct offer with specific ask

**Required Elements**:
- "I would love to work with you"
- "Here's how it works" (solution presentation)
- "Are you ready to get going?" (direct ask)

**Professional Standard**: Must include direct ask for business
**Completion Criteria**: Offer clearly presented with specific call to action

### **Phase 4: Objection Handling**
**Objective**: Professional objection management through 1-3 objections

**Professional Mindset**: "Great salespeople don't even consider the sales call started until they get to the first objection"

**Objection Categories**:
- Timing objections ("I need to think about it")
- Money objections ("It's too expensive")
- Authority objections ("I need to check with...")
- Trust objections ("I'm not sure this will work")

**Avoid**: Only closing prospects who have no objections
**Instead**: Professional objection handling through multiple objections
