    **Problem Identification Hook:**

    "How do you go from [confused state] to [strategic state]? And why does it change everything?"

    **Misconception Challenge Hook:**
    "[Common belief], but something feels off. Can you spot what's wrong?"

    **Direct Question Hook:**
    "Before you [action], did you [critical step]?"

    ## Character Dynamics Framework

    **Newbie Patterns:**
    - Ask audience-proxy questions
    - Represent common misconceptions
    - Experience "aha moments"
    - Show learning progression
    - Commit to better practices

    **Pro Patterns:**
    - Provide strategic insights
    - Challenge assumptions
    - Break fourth wall strategically
    - Guide transformation
    - Deliver authoritative CTAs

    ## Production Optimization

    **Timing Guidelines:**
    - 0-3s: Hook with immediate engagement (1 sentence)
    - 3-8s: Problem articulation with relatability (2 sentences)

    - 20-25s: CTA with value proposition (2 sentences)
